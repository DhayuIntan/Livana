About

"Livana : A Sentiment Analysis of Mobile Banking Service Reviews"

Livana was created in response to the need for in-depth sentiment analysis of user feedback on the Livin by Mandiri application. With over 10 million downloads and more than 542,000 reviews on the Google Play Store, it is essential to thoroughly understand user opinions about the app.

Livana utilizes Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing technology to extract valuable insights from user reviews, assisting the banking industry in improving their services. With Livana, banks can identify areas for improvement, strengthen system advantages, and enhance customer satisfaction, all based on accurate and comprehensive analysis of user sentiment.
